About Me

As a veteran of the IT field, I've spent the last decade developing technical solutions for Windows, Linux, and the Web. During this time, I've worked in construction, hospitality, government, healthcare, defense, social media, and finance. I generally focus on backend automation through CLIs, APIs, systems development and distributed systems. While not my primary focus, I find myself increasingly interested in new programming languages, algorithms, computer graphics, scientific applications, and game development.

Alongside my civlian work I've served and continue to serve the US military in both the Army Reserve and New York Air National Guard. If I'm not working in one of these capacities, it's a good bet that I'm attending to my family and pets, digging through a technical reference, undertaking a hands-on project (usually coding, electronics, radios, networking, systems, or woodworking), or spending time in one of New York's wilderness areas.
